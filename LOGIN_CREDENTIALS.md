## 🔐 Login Process

### General Login Steps:
1. Go to: http://localhost:3000/
2. Enter your department email
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Sign In"
5. You will be redirected to your department dashboard

### Role-Based Access:
- **Admin**: Full system access to all modules
- **Doctors**: Medical modules (OPD, IPD, Prescriptions, Billing)
- **Receptionist**: Patient registration, assignment, bill clearance
- **Nurse**: Nursing station, patient care, vitals monitoring
- **Pharmacist**: Pharmacy POS and inventory
- **Lab Tech**: Laboratory tests and results
- **Radiologist**: Radiology imaging and reports
- **Dentist**: Dental procedures and records
- **Accountant**: Financial management and reports
- **Cashier**: Payment collection and processing
- **HR Manager**: Staff management

---

## 📝 Quick Access Guide

### For Nurses:
1. Login with nurse credentials
2. Navigate to "Nursing Station"
3. View admitted patients
4. Click "Record Care" for any patient
5. Fill vital signs, medications, and observations
6. Save nursing record

### For Receptionists:
1. Login with receptionist credentials
2. Use "Register Patient" tab for new patients
3. Assign patients to departments and doctors
4. Use "Bill Clearance" tab to collect payments

### For Doctors:
1. Login with doctor credentials
2. Access OPD for consultations
3. Go to "Billing" to create patient bills
4. View patient history and records
